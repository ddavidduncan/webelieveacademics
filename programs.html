Programs

Personalized programs that fit your family.

Every program starts with your child — their strengths, their challenges, and what makes them light up. Then we build around that with AI-powered planning and a caring private teacher who makes it real.

1:1 Core Support

Private 1:1 tutoring (K-12 & beyond)

Daily or weekly sessions tailored exactly to your child's needs — homework help, skill mastery, or enrichment with a teacher who truly knows and cares about them.

  • Every subject, every grade — personalized to how your child actually learns
  • A dedicated teacher who explains with patience, celebrates progress, and adjusts live
  • Perfect for students who need consistency, encouragement, and someone in their corner
Full Pathway

Full customized homeschool curriculum

A complete, accredited program built by AI and guided by your child's private teacher — fully flexible, family-friendly, and designed around your child's unique path.

  • Full curriculum across all subjects, sequenced to your child's pace
  • Real structure and accountability without the rigidity of traditional school
  • Teacher guidance plus family visibility into every step of the journey
Advanced Outcomes

Test prep & academic acceleration

SAT, ACT, AP exams, or advanced coursework — with AI-driven practice and real teacher strategy sessions that build both skill and confidence.

  • Targeted preparation focused on your child's specific weak points and strengths
  • Data-informed practice paired with a teacher who knows how to build test-day confidence
  • For ambitious students, high-stakes deadlines, or anyone ready to push further
Habits + Confidence

Executive function & study skills coaching

Help with focus, organization, time management, and confidence — for students who need more than content help. Sometimes the issue isn't what they're learning, but how they're approaching it.

  • Support for getting started, following through, and building real routines
  • Workload and time management that actually sticks
  • Works beautifully alongside tutoring or homeschool programs

What stays true across every program

  • Your child's learning plan is built around them — not a template, not a textbook.
  • A caring teacher who can coach, explain, motivate, and adapt in the moment.
  • Real progress you can see — not vague updates, but genuine growth.
  • Flexibility to change, grow, or shift programs as your child's needs evolve.
