# Competitor Research: TutorCruncher

**Source:** https://help.tutorcruncher.com/en/articles/8420364-tutors-guide-to-tutorcruncher
**Category:** Tutor management / marketplace platform
**Relevance:** Direct competitor to the We Believe School-Market and Teacher Studio

---

## What TutorCruncher Is

TutorCruncher is a tutor management platform used primarily by tutoring agencies and independent tutors. It provides scheduling, invoicing, lesson tracking, and job-board functionality. It is a SaaS backend tool — not a consumer-facing learning platform — meaning it handles operations for tutoring businesses rather than delivering the actual education experience.

**Key contrast with We Believe Academics:** TutorCruncher is an agency management tool. We Believe is a full end-to-end learning platform with AI curriculum generation, a student-facing portal, and a parent dashboard. TutorCruncher has no AI layer, no curriculum engine, and no student learning experience.

---

## Feature Breakdown (Tutor-Side)

### Dashboard
- At-a-glance overview of pending lessons, outstanding invoices, and upcoming schedule
- "Overview" tab shows a 60-day comparison of hours worked vs. earnings
- Action items surfaced directly on the home screen

### Profile & Qualifications
- "My Profile" tab stores contact details, bank information, teaching subjects, qualifications, and documents
- Tutors add qualifications and skills via edit buttons on their profile
- Supports document uploads (single-file upload per job or via a global Documents section)

### Job Applications & Marketplace
- "Available Jobs" browse page with filters: creation date, pay rate, subjects, qualifications
- Tutors apply by submitting a cover letter outlining their suitability
- No algorithmic matching — tutors self-select and apply manually

### Lesson Management
- Lessons added through "My Jobs" section
- Past lessons require a completion report before the client can be billed
- Future lessons show as "Planned"
- Calendar view: switchable between calendar and list format
- Reschedule via drag-and-drop in calendar or Actions > Edit
- Mark lessons complete via the Job summary page

### Online Teaching
- Zoom integration (and other video tools)
- Connection button appears on the lesson page within 24 hours of lesson start time
- No native whiteboard or AI co-pilot during sessions

### Reports & Expenses
- Lesson reports track student progress (manual, tutor-authored)
- Accessible via "Expenses & Reports" tab
- Tutors can submit expenses (travel, books, materials) with supporting document uploads

### Payments & Financial
- "Payment Orders" section shows completed work and earnings breakdown
- "Amount Owed" reflects unpaid lessons and expenses from the past 60 days
- Filter and export options for payment history
- Tax reporting: not explicitly mentioned (We Believe plans 1099-ready reports as a differentiator)

### Availability
- Set via Actions > Set Availability
- "Mass Create" feature for bulk availability scheduling

### Multi-Agency / Global Profile
- Tutors working with multiple agencies can link accounts using the same email
- Single login across multiple agency branches
- Basic information is copied across branches; skills, documents, and qualifications are not shared

## Known Limitations / Pain Points

| Limitation | Implication for We Believe |
|---|---|
| Cannot edit lessons once included on a paid invoice | We Believe should allow corrections with an audit trail |
| Profile access locked if set to "dormant" by agency | We Believe gives teachers full control of their own profile |
| New profiles require company authorization before going live | We Believe onboarding should be self-serve with instant activation |
| No AI curriculum generation or lesson planning | Core We Believe differentiator — draft full units in <60 seconds |
| No student-facing learning portal | We Believe Student Portal is a distinct product layer TutorCruncher lacks entirely |
| No parent dashboard or progress visibility | We Believe Parent Dashboard with SKG heat-map is a direct market gap |
| Manual lesson reports authored by tutor | We Believe AI auto-generates progress narratives; teacher reviews and approves |
| No matching algorithm — tutors self-apply | We Believe 18-factor matching engine is a meaningful differentiator |

---

## Competitive Positioning Summary

TutorCruncher is strong at **operational plumbing** for tutoring agencies (scheduling, invoicing, expense tracking). It is weak on:

1. **Learning experience** — no student portal, no adaptive content, no AI
2. **Parent visibility** — no real-time progress reporting
3. **Teacher support** — no AI lesson co-pilot, no curriculum drafting tools
4. **Direct-to-consumer matching** — marketplace is manual/application-based

We Believe Academics should emphasize these gaps in sales and marketing:
- *"TutorCruncher runs your schedule. We Believe runs your child's education."*
- *"Your tutor logs hours in TutorCruncher. Our teachers see your child's full learning journey."*
