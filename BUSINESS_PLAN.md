# We Believe Academics — Business Plan

---

## 1. Core Mission – The Hybrid Learning Solution for Parents

We Believe Academics exists to deliver the first truly personalized K-12 learning platform that seamlessly fuses frontier AI with vetted, live human teachers. Parents receive one unified dashboard where their child's entire educational journey is customized in real time.

Every child receives:

- **An AI learning companion** that adapts instantly to mastery levels, learning style (visual, auditory, kinesthetic, reading/writing), emotional state (detected via optional sentiment analysis of responses), and long-term retention patterns.
- **A dedicated remote teacher** who is matched not only by subject expertise and grade level, but by teaching philosophy, personality fit, and proven success with similar student profiles.
- **A blended weekly rhythm:** 60–80% AI-driven interactive lessons, projects, and assessments + 20–40% live 1:1 or small-group sessions with the human teacher.

**Result:** Students advance at their own pace (accelerated or remedial), parents regain evenings and weekends, and teachers earn meaningful income without the burnout of traditional classrooms.

---

## 2. The AI "Never-Forgets" Engine – Real-Time Curriculum Generation

At the heart of the platform is a proprietary context layer we call the **Student Knowledge Graph (SKG)**.

### How it works

Every interaction — quiz answer, essay draft, video watch time, hint usage, confidence self-report, even optional webcam emotion cues with explicit parent consent — is captured and encoded into the SKG.

This graph is fed nightly (or on-demand) into frontier large language models (GPT-4o-class or future equivalents) as long-context system prompts. The AI then generates:

- Next-day lesson plans and micro-units
- Adaptive practice sets that target specific knowledge gaps
- Auto-graded but teacher-reviewable assessments (multiple-choice, open-response, project rubrics)
- Real-time pedagogical switches (e.g., from text to interactive simulation if the student is struggling)
- Personalized "growth mindset" nudges and celebration moments

**Privacy-first design:** All data is encrypted at rest and in transit; parents control every data point; no training of base models occurs on student data without explicit opt-in and compensation.

## 3. The Human Teacher Layer – Warmth + Expertise at Scale

Real teachers are not replaced; they are elevated.

### Matching algorithm (v1)

18 weighted factors including subject mastery, years of experience, success metrics with similar demographics, teaching style (Socratic vs. direct instruction vs. project-based), availability, and parent/student feedback scores.

Parents see a short video bio, sample lesson clips, and verified testimonials before booking the first session.

### Teacher role in the platform

- Review AI-generated lesson plans and override or customize any element.
- Join live sessions via integrated video (with screen-share, virtual whiteboard, and real-time AI co-pilot that suggests hints or next questions).
- Provide narrative feedback that the AI then incorporates into future lesson generation.
- Build genuine relationships that research shows are the #1 predictor of long-term student success and love of learning.

## 4. The School-Market – A Transparent Two-Sided Marketplace

Think "LinkedIn + Etsy + Khan Academy" for education.

### For Teachers/Tutors (free to join, two clear paths)

**Path A – "Platform-Only" (recommended for most)**

- $20/month subscription (billed annually = $199)
- Access to the full We Believe tool suite: AI curriculum generator, student SKG, automated billing & invoicing, calendar sync, parent messaging, course builder, white-label student dashboard, analytics, tax-ready 1099 reports.
- Pay only 2% of all billings processed through the platform (covers payment processing + platform maintenance).
- No exclusivity required; teachers keep 100% ownership of their curriculum IP.

**Path B – "Marketplace + Platform"**

- Same tools as Path A.
- Full visibility in the national School-Market directory.
- Ability to create a rich profile: 3-minute intro video, specialty badges (Dyslexia Certified, Gifted & Talented, ESL, STEM Olympiad Coach, etc.), pricing tiers, availability calendar, and success stories.
- Parents can post "Help Wanted" requests in the community feed (e.g., "8th-grade boy with ADHD needs math tutor who also coaches baseball – budget $55/hr"). Teachers respond directly.
- Featured "Story Spotlights" and teacher success podcasts to build personal brands.

### For Parents

- Free search and browsing of all teacher profiles.
- Verified reviews, background checks (via integrated service), and success-rate badges.
- One-click booking and secure payment.
- Ability to invite a favorite neighborhood tutor to join the platform and get the AI tools for free (teacher still pays their own $20/mo if they accept).

## 5. Core Product Features & User Journeys

### Parent Dashboard

- Weekly progress report with visual SKG heat-map
- One-click "Teacher Chat" or "Schedule Session"
- AI-generated recommendations for enrichment activities or summer programs

### Student Portal (ages 5–18, age-appropriate UI)

- Gamified progress rings, streak counters, and choice of avatar companions
- "Ask My AI Tutor Anything" with human-teacher override button
- Portfolio builder that auto-populates with completed projects

### Teacher Studio

- AI lesson co-pilot that drafts full units in under 60 seconds
- Bulk messaging to all current students with one click
- Performance analytics showing which students improved most under their guidance (great for testimonials)
